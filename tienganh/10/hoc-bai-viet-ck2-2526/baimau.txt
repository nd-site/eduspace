The Saola Working Group is a non-profit organisation which was set up in 2006. Its primary aim is to save the saola from disappearing in Laos and Viet Nam. The focus of their work includes several key activities. Firstly, they raise funds for saola protection projects. Secondly, they work with local governments so that they have greater political support for saola protection. Furthermore, they educate people around the world about saolas and the threats to their habitat. Finally, they develop research and protection centres to raise awareness about rare and endangered animals including the saola.
